<bold>Complications</bold></size:large>

Firstly, what if you <bold>don't have a Makefile</bold> ? That's OK, run -

$&gt; linuxdeploy --appdir &lt;some AppDir&gt;

<bold>Using Qt5 or Qt6 ?</bold>

$&gt; linuxdeploy --appdir AppDir --plugin qt --output appimage
